Purpose of This Tool

InstaPrintID is designed exclusively for automotive dealerships and other legitimate businesses that require physical copies of government-issued identification documents for internal recordkeeping and compliance purposes, such as test drive logs and deal jacket documentation.

No Alteration of Identity Information

InstaPrintID does not alter, modify, fabricate, or tamper with any identifying information contained on a driver's license or ID card, including but not limited to names, dates of birth, license numbers, addresses, photographs, expiration dates, or any other personal data.

Output images are geometrically normalized — meaning the image may be cropped, rotated, resized, converted to grayscale, and formatted to standard card dimensions for printing purposes only. This geometric processing is equivalent to standard document scanning and photocopying and does not constitute alteration of identity information.

Not for Identity Verification

Copies produced by InstaPrintID are intended for filing and recordkeeping purposes only. They should not be used as a substitute for identity verification. Users are responsible for verifying the authenticity of any identification document independently.
